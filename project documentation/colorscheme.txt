The below can be helpful for the website

Brand Colours
Primary colour (trust & care)

Deep Teal
HEX: #1F7A7A

Secondary colour (calm support)

Soft Blue
HEX: #6BAED6

Accent colour (warmth)

Soft Green
HEX: #7FBF7F

Neutral background

Light Grey
HEX: #F5F7F7

These colours work well in healthcare and care services.

Typography
Use simple readable fonts.

Headings
Georgia or Libre Baskerville

Body text
Calibri or Open Sans

These look professional and are easy for older readers.

Logo Concept
A good care logo usually includes:

• a heart
• helping hands
• a home symbol

Suggested logo idea
Two hands forming a heart around a small house.

Symbol meaning:

Hands = care
Heart = compassion
House = home care

You can give this to a designer or use Canva.
